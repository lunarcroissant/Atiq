Kolorok

Chess Set

Unique Geometric Design

Kolorok is designed to challenge what chess sets look like. It is supposed to be intriguing and novel.

Crafted from Cement

Cement is rarely used in the objects and devices we use daily, yet we live in it and walk on it every day. The hardline, geometric design of Kolorok fits perfectly with the look and feel of this surprisingly elusive material.

Materials you can feel

Each piece is designed to make you pick it up. The weight and feel of cement combined with the smooth coating and slim felt padding underneath each piece makes for a satisfying move every time.

Coated for Durability

Each piece is sealed with a water-repellent coat for durability and to protect the paint.
